Gabe Arechaederra

Founding Partner

Gabe founded ACRE Investment Company, LLC with a singular purpose: Identify market inefficiencies where a scalable debt and equity solution can take purchase. Prior to founding ACRE Gabe worked at Colliers International for 18 years working his way up from general leasing and sales, institutional investment sales, and private equity and debt markets. He was the founder of the Colliers Private Equity Group in 2003 and maintained a leadership role as the Group’s Director completing in excess of $3.2 billion and over 2.8 million square feet of completed transactions. Prior to Colliers, Gabe attended Loyola High School of Los Angeles and then the University of California at Berkeley.

Allan Chandler

Managing Partner

Allan joined ACRE in 2012 as Managing Partner. He oversees all business operations and financial activities of the company. Allan was a founding member of Golden State Overnight (GSO), a transportation and logistics company with over 20 operations facilities throughout California, Arizona and Nevada. In his 13-year tenure at GSO as Chief Information Officer, Allan provided strategic vision and hands-on management leadership. Prior to joining ACRE, Allan founded a consulting business in 2008 that was focused on developing custom infrastructure design and workflow automation software. Allan is a graduate of the University of Western Ontario, Canada.

Damon Hubbart

Director of Acquisitions

Damon joined ACRE in 2012, he directs the sourcing and acquisition of real estate to specifically fit the unique needs of each tenant. Damon spent 10 years in brokerage with Colliers International in Leasing and Acquisitions. Damon’s experience has provided him with the background and expertise to source, evaluate and close complicated real estate transactions. During his career, Damon has closed in excess of $500 million in transactions including development, leased investments, office, industrial and retail projects. Prior to real estate, Damon had a successful career in enterprise storage technology sales. Damon graduated from the University of Arizona.
